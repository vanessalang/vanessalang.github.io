Vanessa Lang draw's what she wants and it's almost always a weird time

Vanessa Lang is currently an illustration major at The Cleveland Institute of Art. Her art style is best described as illustrative realism, and often features a bizarre twist or humerous take on reality. Her biggest inspiration in her artwork is the children's book author Graeme Base, who was one of the defining influences while developing her own style.

Her preferred mediums are acrylic or digital painting, although she has recently been experimenting with oil painting.
